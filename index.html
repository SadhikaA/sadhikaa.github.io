About Me

I am an incoming senior at Dougherty Valley High School and the #1 Singles player on the DVHS Women’s Varsity Tennis team. My goals are to become a professional in the field of computer science and engineering. I have been passionate about STEM-related courses and projects involving computer science. This has led me to learn different programming languages and participate and run hackathons. My interests in computing are web development, VR technologies, and machine learning. Apart from the above, my hobbies include reading, writing blogs, playing the violin, dancing, and spending time with my family.
